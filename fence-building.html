Our approach

How we build your fence

A thorough, proven installation process that ensures your fence is strong, level, and built to last for years to come.

1
📐

Site survey & layout

We measure your property line, mark post locations, and check for underground utilities before any digging begins — ensuring accuracy and safety from the start.

2
⛏️

Post hole digging

Post holes are dug to the correct depth for Calgary's frost line — ensuring your fence won't shift, lean, or heave through seasonal freeze-thaw cycles.

3
🏗️

Post setting in concrete

Each post is set plumb and level in concrete. We allow adequate curing time before attaching panels to ensure a rock-solid foundation for your fence.

4
🪵

Panel & rail installation

Rails and panels are attached securely and checked for level and alignment throughout. Every board, picket, or panel is fixed for maximum strength and visual appeal.

5
🚪

Gate hanging & hardware

Gates are hung level and plumb with heavy-duty hinges and latches. We test all gate operation to ensure smooth, reliable opening and closing before we leave.

6
🧹

Site cleanup & inspection

We remove all waste materials, excess concrete, and debris. A final walk-around inspection ensures every section meets our quality standards before sign-off.

Got questions?

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our fence building services in Calgary — from materials to timelines and pricing.

How long does fence installation take?
Most standard residential fence installations are completed in 1–2 days. Larger properties or complex terrain may take slightly longer. We'll give you a clear timeline during your free estimate visit so you know exactly what to expect.
What areas in Calgary do you service?
We service all residential and commercial properties throughout Calgary and the surrounding areas including Airdrie, Chestermere, and Cochrane. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific neighbourhood.
Do I need a permit to build a fence in Calgary?
In most cases, fences under 1.85 metres (6 feet) in height do not require a permit in Calgary. Taller fences or those along certain property lines may require approval. We can advise you on permit requirements for your specific project during the assessment.
Will you check for underground utilities before digging?
Absolutely. Before any post holes are dug, we ensure utilities are located to avoid damage. This is a standard part of our site assessment process and is carried out at no additional cost.
Do you remove old fences before installing a new one?
Yes, we offer old fence removal and disposal as part of our full replacement service. This includes removing existing posts, panels, and debris from your property before the new fence installation begins. Ask about this during your quote.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, we are fully licensed and insured for all fence building and installation services in Calgary. You can have complete peace of mind that your property and our team are protected throughout the entire project.
How much does fence installation cost in Calgary?
Fence installation costs depend on the material, length, height, and site conditions. We provide a free, no-obligation quote after assessing your property. Our pricing is fully transparent — no hidden fees and what we quote is what you pay.
What is included in the quoted price?
Your quote includes all materials, labour, post-hole digging, concrete, and property cleanup after installation. Gate installation and hardware are quoted separately unless included in your package. We itemize everything so there are no surprises.
Do you offer any discounts?
Yes. We offer competitive pricing for larger projects and returning customers. If you are bundling our fence building service with lawn care or moving services, ask us about multi-service discounts. Call or contact us for details.
How do I get a free quote?
Simply call us at +1(825)736-6306 or fill out the contact form on this page. We'll arrange a free site assessment at your convenience — one of our fence specialists will visit your property and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote completely free of charge.
What types of fences do you build?
We install wood (pressure-treated pine and cedar), vinyl (privacy, picket, and semi-privacy), and chain-link (galvanized and vinyl-coated) fences for residential and commercial properties. We also install gates and locking hardware to match any fence style.
Which fence material is best for Calgary's climate?
All materials we use are suited to Calgary's climate. Pressure-treated wood handles freeze-thaw cycles well and is the most popular choice. Vinyl is completely weatherproof and never needs painting. Chain-link is highly durable and ideal for larger areas. We'll help you choose the best option during your assessment.
How long will a wood fence last?
A properly installed, pressure-treated wood fence typically lasts 15–20 years with basic maintenance (occasional staining or sealing). Cedar fences are naturally rot-resistant and can last even longer with proper care. We'll advise on the best maintenance schedule for your fence type.
Does vinyl fencing require any maintenance?
Vinyl fencing requires very little maintenance. It never needs painting, staining, or sealing. An occasional rinse with a garden hose is all that's needed to keep it looking like new. Vinyl is an excellent long-term investment for homeowners who prefer a low-maintenance option.
Can you build a custom fence design?
Yes! We can accommodate custom heights, decorative post caps, angled terrain, and mixed materials. During your free site assessment, we'll discuss your specific design preferences and what's possible within your property layout and budget.
How deep do you set fence posts in Calgary?
In Calgary, posts must be set below the frost line to prevent heaving. We dig post holes to the appropriate depth for your fence height and soil type — typically 3–4 feet — and set each post in concrete for maximum stability and longevity.
