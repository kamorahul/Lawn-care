Got questions?

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about fence building in Calgary — from permits to pricing to post depth.

When is the best time to build a fence in Calgary?
We build fences year-round, but spring through fall is the busiest period. The ground needs to be thawed for post-setting. We typically book 1–3 weeks out during peak season, so contact us early if you have a target date.
How high can I build a fence in Calgary?
In most Calgary residential areas, backyard fences can be up to 6 feet (1.8m) tall without a permit. Front yard and corner lot rules vary by community. We're happy to guide you on the specifics for your address.
Who owns the fence between two houses?
In Alberta, boundary fences are typically a shared responsibility between neighbours. Both property owners share equal ownership of the fence on the property line. We recommend confirming your property boundaries using your Real Property Report before installation.
Do I need a permit to build a fence in Calgary?
For standard 4' or 6' backyard fences, you typically don't need a permit in Calgary. However, taller fences, fences on corner lots, or fences near utility right-of-ways may require approval. We'll flag anything permit-related during your site inspection.
What is the lifespan of a fence in Calgary?
A properly installed pressure-treated fence in Calgary typically lasts 20 to 30 years. Cedar can last just as long with occasional oiling or staining. Our deep-set concrete posts (42–48 inches) are a major factor in fence longevity in freeze-thaw climates.
Can you remove my old fence before building a new one?
Yes — we offer fence removal and disposal as an add-on service. Just let us know when requesting your quote. Old fence material is disposed of responsibly. We can usually remove and replace in the same visit for most projects.
What is the cost per foot to build a new fence in Calgary?
For a standard pressure-treated fence with 4"×4" posts at 4' tall, pricing starts at $45 per linear foot. A 6' tall fence typically starts at $50 per linear foot. Cedar fences with 6"×6" posts are closer to $70 per linear foot. Use our calculator above for a specific estimate.
What is included in my quoted project cost?
Your quote includes all labour, materials (boards, posts, concrete, fasteners), delivery, installation, and site cleanup. Gates are itemized separately. The only things not included are permit fees (if applicable) and fence removal unless added on.
How do I get an estimate for my fence project?
Start with our online calculator above for a ballpark figure. Then click "Request a free quote" and one of our specialists will reach out to schedule a free, no-obligation site inspection to confirm measurements and finalize pricing.
What is the most affordable material and design?
Pressure-treated wood with 4"×4" posts in a Stockade style is the most budget-friendly option. It's durable, effective, and looks great. We never sacrifice quality for cost — even our base option uses hand-picked lumber and full-depth concrete footings.
What materials can you build a fence with?
We primarily build with pressure-treated wood, cedar, and composite. Each has different price points, aesthetics, and maintenance requirements. Our team will help you choose the right material for your budget and goals during the site inspection.
Can you build a cedar fence?
Absolutely. Cedar is a premium natural material that resists rot and insects without chemical treatment. It ages beautifully to a silver-gray patina, or you can stain/oil it to preserve the warm wood tone. Cedar fences with 6"×6" posts start around $70/linear foot.
What is pressure-treated wood?
Pressure-treated lumber is wood that has been infused with preservatives under pressure to protect against rot, decay, and insect damage. It's the standard material for outdoor structures in Calgary and carries a long service life when properly installed with concrete footings.
How long does it take to complete a fence?
Our crews build at approximately 70 linear feet per day. A typical Calgary backyard project (100–150 ft) takes 2–3 days. Larger projects or those with complex terrain may take slightly longer. Your exact timeline is confirmed before we break ground.
What fence styles can you install?
We build Stockade, Bastion, Fortress, Horizontal, and Shadowbox styles as standard offerings. We also do custom designs including ornamental detailing, windows/arbours, dog runs, privacy screens, and modern post-and-rail systems. If you can draw it, we can build it.
Is lumber included in your warranty?
Our 1-year workmanship warranty covers all labour and installation defects — posts shifting, boards coming loose, gate hardware issues, etc. Lumber itself is a natural material and not covered under the warranty, but we hand-select our boards to minimize any quality concerns from the start.
