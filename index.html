Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our lawn care, packing & moving, and fence building services in Calgary.

What lawn care services do you offer?
We offer full lawn maintenance including mowing, edging, weed removal, fertilizing, leaf cleanup, and seasonal treatments. Whether it's a one-time clean-up or regular maintenance, we've got you covered.
How often should I get my lawn maintained?
We recommend weekly or bi-weekly mowing during the growing season and monthly visits in fall. Our team will suggest the best schedule based on your lawn type and property size.
Do you supply all packing materials for moving?
Yes! We bring everything — boxes, bubble wrap, shrink wrap, packing tape, and furniture covers. All materials are included in your quote so there are no surprise costs.
How long does a home move take?
Most residential moves are completed in a single day. A 1–2 bedroom home typically takes 3–5 hours, while a 4+ bedroom home may take 8–10 hours depending on distance and volume of items.
What types of fences do you build?
We build wood, vinyl, and chain-link fences for residential and commercial properties. All fences are custom-built to your specifications and installed by our experienced team.
How long does fence installation take?
Most standard fence installations are completed in 1–2 days depending on the length and type. We'll give you a clear timeline during your free estimate visit.
Are your services licensed and insured?
Yes, we are fully licensed and insured for all services — lawn care, packing & moving, and fence building. You can have complete peace of mind when our team is working on your property.
How do I get a free quote?
Simply call us at ☎+1(825)736-6306 or fill out our contact form. We'll visit your property, assess the job, and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote — completely free of charge.
