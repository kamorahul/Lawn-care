Got questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our professional packing services in Calgary.

What types of items do you pack?
We pack everything — kitchenware, electronics, clothing, furniture, artwork, fragile collectibles, gym equipment, mattresses, and more. Our team is trained to properly wrap and protect every item type, no matter how delicate or bulky.
Do you provide all the packing materials?
Yes! We bring all necessary packing materials including double-walled boxes (various sizes), bubble wrap, packing paper, shrink wrap, mattress covers, moving blankets, tape, and specialty boxes for TVs and mirrors. Everything is included in your package price.
How long does professional packing take?
Most homes can be fully packed in one day. A 1–2 bedroom home typically takes 3–5 hours, while a 3–4 bedroom home may take 6–8 hours. We'll give you an accurate time estimate during your free consultation based on your specific inventory.
Can I book packing without moving?
Absolutely! Our packing service is available as a standalone booking. We can pack your home and leave everything ready for you to move yourself, or coordinate with another moving company. There's no requirement to use our moving service.
What areas in Calgary do you service?
We service all neighbourhoods throughout Calgary and surrounding areas including Airdrie, Chestermere, Cochrane, and Okotoks. We also handle long-distance packing and moving across Alberta. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.
Can I cancel or reschedule?
Yes, we understand plans change. We ask for at least 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid fees. We'll always do our best to accommodate your new preferred date and time with as little disruption as possible.
How much does professional packing cost in Calgary?
Packing costs depend on your home size, the number of items, and the services selected. We offer a free, no-obligation quote after your consultation. Our pricing is fully transparent — no hidden fees. What we quote is what you pay, period.
Are packing materials included in the price?
Yes. All packing materials — boxes, bubble wrap, tape, mattress covers, packing paper, and specialty boxes — are included in our full packing packages. There are no additional material charges added after the fact.
Do you offer discounts for larger homes?
Yes! We offer volume discounts for larger homes and competitive rates for combined packing + moving packages. Returning customers also receive loyalty pricing. Contact us to discuss the best deal for your specific situation.
How do I get a free packing quote?
Fill out the quote form on this page or call us at 587-962-1919. We'll review your needs and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote — completely free. We typically respond within the hour during business hours.
Do you have experience packing fragile items?
Yes — it's one of our specialties. Our team is trained in professional wrapping techniques for glassware, china, artwork, electronics, and antiques. We use the correct materials for each item type and document fragile items for extra care during transport.
Can you do partial packing (only some rooms)?
Absolutely. Partial packing is very common — many clients pack their own items and have us handle just the kitchen, fragile items, or specific rooms. We'll work with exactly what you need and quote only for those areas.
What should I do to prepare for packing day?
We recommend setting aside any personal documents, medications, or valuables you want to transport yourself. Clear pathways throughout the home and let us know about any access restrictions. Otherwise, leave the packing to us — we'll handle everything.
Do you disassemble furniture as part of packing?
Yes. Furniture disassembly and reassembly is available as part of our packing service or as a standalone add-on. We bring all necessary tools and bag all hardware separately so reassembly at your new home is fast and easy.
Can you pack specialty items like pianos or gym equipment?
Yes. We have experience with specialty items including pianos, pool tables, safes, large gym equipment, and mounted TVs. These require specific handling and materials — let us know during your consultation so we can plan and quote accordingly.
What items can you NOT pack?
For safety reasons we cannot pack hazardous materials (paint, propane, chemicals, ammunition), perishable food, plants, or live animals. We recommend making separate arrangements for these before packing day. If you're unsure about a specific item, just ask.
