Our approach

How we build your wooden fence

A proven installation process ensuring your wooden fence is strong, level, and built to last for decades.

1
📐

Site survey & layout

We measure your property line, mark post locations, and check for underground utilities before any digging begins — ensuring accuracy and safety.

2
⛏️

Post hole digging

Post holes are dug below Calgary's frost line to ensure your wooden fence won't shift or heave through seasonal freeze-thaw cycles.

3
🏗️

Post setting in concrete

Each post is set plumb and level in concrete. We allow full curing time before attaching rails and boards for a rock-solid foundation.

4
🪵

Rail & board installation

Rails are attached and boards are fixed with precision — checked for level and alignment throughout for maximum strength and visual appeal.

5
🚪

Gate hanging & hardware

Wooden gates are hung with heavy-duty hinges and latches. We test all gate operation to ensure smooth, reliable use before we leave.

6
🧹

Site cleanup & inspection

We remove all waste materials and debris. A final walk-around inspection ensures every section meets our quality standards before sign-off.

Got questions?

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our wooden fence installation services in Calgary.

How long does wooden fence installation take?
Most standard residential wooden fence installations are completed in 1–2 days. We'll give you a clear timeline during your free site assessment.
What areas in Calgary do you service?
We service all residential and commercial properties throughout Calgary and surrounding areas including Airdrie, Chestermere, and Cochrane.
Do I need a permit for a wooden fence in Calgary?
In most cases, fences under 1.85 metres (6 feet) do not require a permit in Calgary. We can advise on permit requirements during your assessment.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, we are fully licensed and insured for all wooden fence installation services in Calgary. Your property and our team are fully protected throughout the project.
What types of wood do you use?
We use premium pressure-treated pine and cedar. Both are suited to Calgary's climate — pressure-treated for maximum durability and cedar for natural rot resistance and beauty.
How long will a wooden fence last?
A properly installed pressure-treated wood fence typically lasts 15–20 years. Cedar can last even longer with occasional staining or sealing. We'll advise on the best maintenance plan for your fence.
Does a wooden fence need maintenance?
Wooden fences benefit from occasional staining or sealing every 2–3 years to maintain their appearance and extend their lifespan. We also offer staining and sealing services.
