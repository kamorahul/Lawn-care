Got questions?

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our lawn care services in Calgary — from scheduling to pricing.

How often should I have my lawn mowed?
During the growing season (typically May through September in Calgary), we recommend weekly or bi-weekly mowing depending on your grass type and growth rate. We'll assess your lawn and suggest the best frequency during your free site visit.
What areas in Calgary do you service?
We service all residential and commercial properties throughout Calgary and surrounding areas including Airdrie, Chestermere, and Cochrane. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific neighbourhood.
Do I need to be home when you visit?
No, you don't need to be home. As long as we have access to your lawn, our crew will complete the service and notify you when it's done. We'll discuss access requirements during your initial assessment.
What happens if it rains on my scheduled service day?
We monitor the weather closely and reschedule services when conditions are unsuitable for lawn care. We'll contact you in advance to arrange an alternative time that works for you. Wet lawns can be damaged by mowing, so we never cut in poor conditions.
Are your lawn care services eco-friendly?
Yes. We use environmentally responsible fertilizers and weed control products that are safe for children, pets, and local wildlife. Our team is trained in responsible application practices to protect Calgary's natural environment.
Can I cancel or change my service plan?
Absolutely. You can adjust or cancel your service plan at any time with reasonable notice. We believe in flexible, customer-first arrangements — no lock-in contracts or cancellation penalties.
How much does lawn mowing cost in Calgary?
Lawn mowing prices vary based on your property size, terrain complexity, and the frequency of service. We provide a free, no-obligation quote after assessing your lawn. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees — what we quote is what you pay.
What is included in the quoted price?
Your quote includes all labour, equipment, and time for the services selected. Fertilizer and weed control products are included in seasonal treatment plans. There are no additional charges for standard travel within our service area.
Do you offer discounts for regular customers?
Yes! Customers on recurring weekly or bi-weekly service plans receive better rates than one-time visits. We also offer loyalty discounts for customers who book multiple services (e.g., mowing + fertilizing + cleanup) in a single package.
How do I get a free quote?
Simply call us at 587-962-1919 or fill out the contact form on this page. We'll arrange a free site assessment where one of our lawn specialists will visit your property and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote — completely free.
What lawn care services do you offer?
We offer a full range of lawn care services including: lawn mowing and edging, fertilizing and weed control, spring and fall cleanups, core aeration and overseeding, hedge and shrub trimming, and full-season property maintenance packages.
What is core aeration and do I need it?
Core aeration involves removing small plugs of soil to reduce compaction and allow water, air, and nutrients to penetrate the root zone. If your lawn is heavily trafficked, has thatch buildup, or drains poorly, aeration (ideally combined with overseeding) can dramatically improve grass health.
When is the best time to fertilize a lawn in Calgary?
The best times to fertilize in Calgary are early spring (after the last frost), mid-summer, and late fall before the ground freezes. Our seasonal treatment plans are timed to align with these optimal windows for the best results.
Do you do commercial lawn care as well?
Yes! We service both residential and commercial properties in Calgary. Whether it's a single-family home, strata complex, office park, or retail property, we can design a lawn maintenance program to fit your needs and budget. Contact us for a commercial quote.
Can you repair a damaged or patchy lawn?
Absolutely. Lawn repair services include overseeding bare patches, soil amendment, and targeted weed or pest treatment to address the root cause of damage. We'll assess the issue during your free site visit and recommend the most effective solution.
How long does a typical lawn visit take?
Service duration depends on your property size and the services being performed. A standard mow, edge, and blow for an average residential lawn typically takes 30–60 minutes. Full cleanup or treatment visits may take longer. We'll give you an accurate estimate upfront.
